---
description: Build a compact map of the current repo or topic folder
---

Use this when the folder is unfamiliar, the task is broad, or research needs orientation before reading files.

Run:
`bash ./scripts/repo-map.sh ${ARGUMENTS:-.}`

Then respond compactly with:
- the important control files
- the main content areas
- the most relevant headings or symbols
- what to inspect next

Do not treat the map as proof. It is a navigation aid before targeted reading.
